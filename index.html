Welcome to FIBA, the International Basketball Federation.

Our mission is to promote and develop the game of basketball around the world. With 212 National Basketball Federations and over 450 million players and fans, we're dedicated to uniting the global basketball community.

Explore the exciting world of basketball with us, from international competitions like the FIBA Basketball World Cup to the rules that govern the sport. Whether you're a player, a fan, or just curious about the game, FIBA is your ultimate source for everything basketball.
